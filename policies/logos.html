LuzFaltex Logos and Usage

Below you will find a way to access our trademarks and other logos. Be sure to read the terms before downloading or using any of our trademarks.

Do

  • Use our logos to link to LuzFaltex or any of its projects.
  • Use our logos to advertise that your project is run in partnership with LuzFaltex.
  • Use our logos in a blog post or news article about LuzFaltex or its projects.

Don't

  • Use our logos for or in the logo for your project or product.
  • Create a modified version of our logos.
  • Integrate our logos into your own logo.
  • Use any LuzFaltex artwork without permission.
  • Sell any LuzFaltex artwork without permission.
  • Change the colors, dimensions, or add your own text/images.

Please contact us

  • If you want to use artwork not included in this repository
  • If you want to use these images in a video/mainstream media

Naming projects and products

Please avoid naming your project anything that implies LuzFaltex's endorsement. This also applies to domain names.
