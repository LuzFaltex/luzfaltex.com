DMCA Counter Notice

If you believe your content on a LuzFaltex project was mistakenly disabled by a DMCA takedown request, you have the right to contest the takedown by submitting a counter-notice. IF you do, we will wait 10-14 days and re-enable your content unless the copyright owner initiates legal action against you before then.

The form below includes information that LuzFaltex needs in order to process a counter notice to a DMCA takedown request. Before you begin, please read LuzFaltex's Guide to Submitting a DMCA Counter-Notice.
